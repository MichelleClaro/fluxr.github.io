Generative Research

The purpose of "generative research" is to gain empathy for your users, to discover their needs and goals. This is separate from evaluative testing which measures the product's performance.

This process can typically be conducted within a 1 week design sprint, or longer depending on the breadth of the project.

Process

1

Plan

Create a research plan that will inform your process & strategy for your work
2

Gather

Giving you the context you need to understand your design challenge
3

Recruit

Find participants to help you uncover the right needs and goals
4

Craft

A great script will help you ellicit stories, explore emotions and get to the "why"
5

Conduct

How to confidently facilitate customer interviews to get to the insights you need
6

Synthesize

Package and relay your learnings back to your team
