Evaluative Research

The purpose of the "evaluative test" phase is to focus on the product's usability and inductiveness for customers.

This qualitative research will seek to validate or dispel product design assumptions during 1:1 sessions, we ask users to think-aloud as they test given scenerios.

Process

1

Draft

Build a test script that's based on scenarios and follow-up with why
2

Recruit

Gather 4-8 customers from specific segments the test your hypothesis
3

Conduct

Facilitate test interviews to uncover qualitative actionable insights
4

Synthesize

A great script will help you ellicit stories, explore emotions and get to the "why"

Other Research Methods

  • Surveys and Questionnaires
  • Customer Interviews
  • Ethnographic studies
  • Diary studies
  • Card Sorting
