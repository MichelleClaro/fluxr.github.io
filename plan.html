What is the "Plan" stage?

This is the beginning of your generative research. Here, you're solving for a lack of information. Plan to define the goals and methods for your research study. Great plans will act as a reference guide and help you along with the rest of the research steps.

1) Determine why you need to research

  • What do you/your team want to learn from the research?
  • What do/don't you know about your customers?
  • Who the research is going to benefit. Who are you designing for?
  • Which stakeholders/team members should be involved

2) Invite your stakeholders. This may include:

  • Product Managers
  • Development Team Leads
  • Designers/Researchers
